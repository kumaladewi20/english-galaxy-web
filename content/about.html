About Us

English Galaxy is a dynamic and user-friendly English learning course that offers interactive lessons and resources for learners at all levels. It provides an effective and engaging way to improve English proficiency, making it suitable for both beginners and advanced learners.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Indonesia by providing accessible, effective, and engaging English language education, fostering global communication and personal growth.

Our Vision

Our is to redefine English language education in Indonesia, making it accessible and transformative for all, fostering universal communication and personal growth.
