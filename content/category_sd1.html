Introduction to Basic English Vocabullary

Basic English vocabulary is very important for good communication in the English language. If you're starting to learn English or want to improve your language skills, it's essential to learn these important words and phrases first. Here's a simple introduction to some basic English vocabulary categories:

1. Greetings and Social Expressions:

• Hello
• Hi
• Good morning
• Good afternoon
• Good evening
• How are you?
• Thank you

2. Numbers:

• One
• Two
• Three

2. Numbers:
