Sekolah Menengah Atas (SMA)

Recount Text

Characteristics of Recount Text:

• Utilizes the past tense.
• Includes phrases that describe when and where events occurred.
• Incorporates conjunctions like "first," "then," and "next."

Varieties of Recount Text:

1. Factual
This type of text recounts real events or factual occurrences, such as a police report or a historical incident.
2. Personal
As the author, you certainly have personal experiences, and your written account of those experiences falls under the category of personal recount text.
3. Imaginative
Similar to what was previously discussed, imaginative recount text closely resembles narrative text. However, the distinction lies in the fact that imaginative recount text recounts imaginative experiences, such as dreams.
